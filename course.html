The BA (Hons) in Culinary Arts Course

The BA (Hons) in Culinary Arts Course is a full-time, four-year, level 8 program that offers innovative and dynamic culinary education. It revises traditional culinary skills training by incorporating a variety of other disciplines, such as food innovation, media and visual arts, food policy, speciality food production, sustainable practices, and gastronomy.

The curriculum is designed to expose students to interdisciplinary learning, research, and enquiry, with less emphasis on training and more on holistic development. This approach prepares graduates to elevate current industry practices to a new dimension both regionally and nationally. Graduates will be empowered to become self-starters with passion and entrepreneurial traits.

International and Industrial Experience

International and industrial experience is a central component of the degree. In Year 3 of the program, students spend a semester studying at one of our partner colleges in Canada, France, Northern Ireland, Spain, The Netherlands, or the USA. They also complete a semester on international work placement in a country of their choice. This opportunity provides students with first-hand experience of international standards and best practices in culinary operations, equipping them with the skills necessary to excel in the global workplace.

Interdisciplinary Study

The course offers interdisciplinary study of social, human, and culinary sciences, encouraging students to think about the future of gastronomy. Students explore the connections between environmental consciousness and creativity, politics and food production, the media and culinary arts, among other topics.




Modules

Our program is designed to equip students with the skills and knowledge necessary to excel in the culinary industry. From foundational courses in financial accounting and advanced culinary skills, to specialized electives in entrepreneurship and food innovation, we offer a comprehensive curriculum to help students achieve their career goals. We also offer opportunities for academic study abroad and industrial placements, allowing you to gain real-world experience and explore different cultures. Opportunities

Graduates of the Bachelor of Arts (Hons) in Culinary Arts program are equipped with interdisciplinary skills and knowledge that open up a wide range of career opportunities in the culinary sector and other industries. These include:

  • Management positions within the hospitality sector, such as specialist chef, chef de partie, and sous chef
  • Marketing and promotion
  • Entrepreneurship in creative food technology and development industries
  • Food product innovation centers
  • Artisan food production units
  • Food promotion
  • Education and training
  • Event catering and food and beverage retail management
  • In the domain of food styling and food and related journalism

Graduates can also opt to pursue a Master's degree to further expand their skill set.

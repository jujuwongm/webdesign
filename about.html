The Waterford Institute of Technology's Department of Culinary Arts offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in culinary arts, hospitality management, and tourism. The department is equipped with state-of-the-art facilities, including a purpose-built demonstration theatre and commercial kitchens, which provide students with hands-on training in all aspects of culinary arts and hospitality management.

The department's faculty members are experienced industry professionals and renowned chefs who bring their expertise and passion to the classroom. The curriculum is designed to provide students with a broad understanding of culinary arts, hospitality management, and tourism, as well as specialized skills in their area of interest. The department also emphasizes sustainability and ethical sourcing in its approach to culinary education.

The Waterford Institute of Technology's Department of Culinary Arts has a strong reputation in the culinary industry, and its graduates are highly sought after by top employers in the hospitality and tourism sector.




Our mission

At the Waterford Institute of Technology's Department of Culinary Arts, our mission is to provide a world-class culinary education that prepares our students for successful careers in the hospitality and tourism industry. We strive to cultivate a passion for culinary arts and hospitality management, while also emphasizing innovation, sustainability, and ethical practices. Our faculty members are experienced industry professionals who bring their expertise and passion to the classroom, and our state-of-the-art facilities provide students with hands-on training in all aspects of culinary arts and hospitality management. Through our programs, we aim to instill in our students the skills, knowledge, and values necessary to become leaders in their field, and to make a positive impact on the culinary industry and society as a whole.


Our history

For over 30 years, the Culinary Arts Department of the Waterford Institute of Technology has been a leading provider of culinary education in Ireland. Since the 1990s, the department has been expanding its program, building state-of-the-art facilities, and hiring experienced industry professionals to teach and mentor students. The department has earned a strong reputation in the culinary industry for its innovative approach to culinary education and commitment to sustainability and ethical practices. Its graduates are highly sought after by top employers in the hospitality and tourism sector, and many have gone on to become successful chefs, restaurateurs, and entrepreneurs.
